Tournament Banned Heros

In Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), heroes are banned during the draft pick phase to prevent the opposing team from selecting them, as well as to ensure a balanced and strategic match. Players ban heroes they believe are too strong, or to counter the enemy team's potential composition.
For Example: A team might ban a hero like Lolita, Hylos or Argus if they are strong in the current meta or if they are strong against the enemy team's composition.
